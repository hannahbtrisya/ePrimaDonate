About Us

Empowering Community through Transparent and Digital Giving

About Masjid Prima Saujana Kajang

Masjid Prima Saujana Kajang is a beacon for the Kajang community — offering spiritual guidance, education, and charity programs. With strong roots in Islamic values, our mosque is committed to uplift the lives of all, especially the underprivileged.

Why Digital Donation?

In a digital age, contributing to charity must be just as easy. Our system lets you donate securely, track history, and support specific campaigns. It's efficient, transparent, and always open — just like the spirit of giving.

Our Mission

💡 Foster a caring, connected community 📊 Ensure sustainable funding for mosque operations 🤝 Empower youth, education, and outreach 📋 Provide accountability through digital records
