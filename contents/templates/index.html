About Masjid Prima Saujana Kajang

Masjid Prima Saujana Kajang is a community-centered mosque dedicated to serving the spiritual and social needs of the Kajang community. Through various donation campaigns, we aim to sustain mosque operations, support the underprivileged, and empower local education and development programs.

Donation Types

Mosque Operational Fund

Support utility bills, staff salaries, and daily activities at the mosque.

Mosque Development Fund

Help fund expansion projects, new facilities, and structural repairs.

Education & Religious Classes

Contribute to Islamic kindergarten, Quran lessons, and community education programs.

Asnaf Food Aid

Provide monthly food support to underprivileged families around Kajang.
